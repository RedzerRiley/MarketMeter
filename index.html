Philippines Consumer Price Tool

Shop Smarter.

MarketMeter is a community-driven platform that lets Filipino consumers check, submit, and compare real market prices of basic goods against government-issued Suggested Retail Prices (SRP). Know what you should pay before you pay it.

Get Started View Prices

Why MarketMeter?

Built for everyday Filipino consumers who deserve price transparency in their local markets.

Transparent Pricing

Access real, community-submitted prices from markets across the Philippines. No guesswork, no hidden markups. Just honest data to help you budget better and spend wisely.

Community Verified Data

Every price submission goes through community confirmation before it appears publicly. This ensures that the data you see reflects what is actually happening in local wet markets and grocery stores.

SRP Comparison

MarketMeter integrates official Suggested Retail Prices from the Department of Trade and Industry so you can immediately see when a merchant is selling above the regulated maximum.

How It Works

A simple, transparent three-step process powered by the community.

01

Users Submit Prices

Registered community members submit the prices they encounter in their local markets, wet markets, and grocery stores. Each entry includes the product, price, location, and date of purchase.

02

Community Confirms Entries

Other community members in the same area review and confirm submitted prices. This peer verification process filters out inaccurate or outdated entries, maintaining data integrity.

03

Compare with SRP Instantly

Once confirmed, prices are displayed alongside the official DTI Suggested Retail Price. Consumers can immediately see whether a price is fair, above SRP, or below market average.
